# Engagement Models

I offer three ways to collaborate via Upwork, depending on your project needs:

### 1. Fixed-Price Projects
Best for well-defined scopes (e.g., "Migrate from Heroku to AWS" or "Build a CI/CD pipeline for our React app").
* Requires a strict Statement of Work (SOW).
* Milestones are funded upfront via Upwork Escrow.
* Includes 2 rounds of minor revisions after delivery.

### 2. Hourly Engagements
Best for ongoing development, infrastructure refactoring, or staff augmentation.
* Tracked transparently via the Upwork Desktop App.
* Weekly check-ins to ensure hours align with business priorities.

### 3. Monthly Retainer (Maintenance & SLA)
Best for clients who need guaranteed response times for production infrastructure.
* Fixed weekly/monthly Upwork stipend.
* Includes monitoring, patching, and emergency response (See SLA doc).
