# The Onboarding Process

Once a contract is initiated on Upwork, we follow a strict 4-step onboarding process to ensure immediate momentum.

* **Day 1: The Kickoff Call.** A 30-45 minute meeting to align on architecture, business goals, and timelines.
* **Day 2: Access & Credentials.** I will send you a list of required IAM permissions, GitHub access, and cloud console invites. (See `access-requirements.md`).
* **Day 3-5: Audit & Architecture.** I will review your current setup and provide a high-level Architecture Diagram for your approval.
* **Day 5+: Execution.** Daily or bi-weekly updates on progress until delivery.
