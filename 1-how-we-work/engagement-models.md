# 🤝 Engagement Models & Pricing Structure

Every project is unique. To ensure we are fully aligned on budget, scope, and deliverables, I offer three distinct engagement models through Upwork. 

By clearly defining how we work together, we eliminate billing surprises and ensure the focus remains on delivering high-quality infrastructure.

---

## 1. ⏱️ Hourly Contracts
**Best for:** Ongoing development, open-ended tasks, troubleshooting, or projects where the scope is expected to change.

*   **How it works:** Time is tracked using the Upwork Desktop App (or logged manually by mutual agreement) for the hours I actively work on your infrastructure.
*   **Flexibility:** You can increase, decrease, or pause the weekly hourly limit at any time.
*   **Transparency:** You only pay for the exact time spent working, consulting, or documenting.
*   **Ideal Use Cases:** CI/CD pipeline optimization, ongoing cloud cost optimization, server migrations with unknown variables, and general DevOps consulting.

## 2. 🎯 Fixed-Price Projects
**Best for:** Projects with a well-defined scope, clear deliverables, and a strict timeline.

*   **How it works:** We agree on a total project cost upfront. The project is then divided into functional milestones. Funds are placed in Upwork Escrow and released as each milestone is completed and approved.
*   **Predictability:** You know the exact cost of the project before we write a single line of code.
*   **Revisions:** Fixed-price contracts include a predetermined number of minor architectural revisions (usually 2) per milestone. *Note: Major changes to the original scope will require a new milestone or a shift to an hourly model.*
*   **Ideal Use Cases:** Building a new Terraform module, setting up a fresh Kubernetes cluster, or an initial security audit.

## 3. 🛡️ Monthly Maintenance / Retainer
**Best for:** Production environments that require ongoing monitoring, security patching, and SLA-backed emergency support.

*   **How it works:** Set up via Upwork's recurring weekly billing or a monthly fixed-price contract. 
*   **Guarantees:** Guarantees my availability for emergency incident response (see my[SLA terms](./service-level-agreement.md) for response times).
*   **Proactive Work:** Includes routine maintenance such as updating Helm charts, rotating IAM keys, patching OS vulnerabilities, and reviewing AWS/GCP bills to prevent overspending.
*   **Ideal Use Cases:** Post-launch support, managing production Kubernetes clusters, or acting as your fractional DevOps engineer.

---

### 💡 Not sure which model is right for you?
Don't worry! During our initial kickoff call, I will listen to your requirements and recommend the engagement model that makes the most financial and operational sense for your business. All contracts and payments are strictly processed through **Upwork** for our mutual protection.
