# Code of Conduct & Professional Integrity

As a freelance DevOps Engineer, I act as an extension of your team. I hold myself to the following standards:

1. **Zero-Downtime Philosophy:** Production changes are planned, tested, and executed with rollback strategies.
2. **Extreme Ownership:** If a pipeline fails or an deployment breaks on my watch, I own it and fix it.
3. **No Vendor Lock-in (Where Possible):** I advocate for open-source and cloud-agnostic tools like Terraform and Kubernetes unless native tools (like CloudFormation) better suit your specific business needs.
4. **Transparent Communication:** I will never leave you guessing about the status of a project. Updates are provided consistently via Upwork or your preferred team chat.
